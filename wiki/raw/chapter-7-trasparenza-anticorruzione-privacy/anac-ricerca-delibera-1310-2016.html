Search type

Search is conducted both on the content of the pages hosting the documents and within the documents themselves.

You can perform a targeted search for acts and publications from the Consult documents section, or a broader search across the entire site using the Free search.

The results are listed in decreasing order of relevance, i.e. based on how often and where the keywords appear within the document.
In Search Type section, you can refine your search using the criteria Exact phrase, At least one of the words or All words.

Search Type
The search type can operate by searching for:
•   At least one of the words
•   All words
•   Exact phrase

Sorting Results
You can sort the search results by selecting one of the following options:
Sort by
•   Most relevant: results are ranked based on how closely they match the search terms;
•   Most recent: results are ordered by publication date.

Content filters:
•   Year: the calendar year in which the act was issued;
•   Subject: such as Anti-corruption, Public contracts, Transparency;
•   Categories: thematic area related to the act;
•   Typology: type of act;
•   Stakeholder typology: Public Administrations, Citizens, Companies
•   Date (from/to): possibility of filtering the contents by entering a start date and end date of publication of the content searched for, or you can also use quick filters like alternatively use the shortcuts Today, Last week, Last month

Include/Extend attachments from the search
At the bottom of the page, you can choose to include or exclude document attachments in your search by clicking the Extend/Exclude attachments from search button.
