# Editorial formatting research

## Date
2026-05-10

## Decision
Use a professional manual-workbook format for `Il Metodo BANDO`.

Primary specification:

```text
Trim: 17 x 24 cm circa
KDP equivalent: 6.69 x 9.61 in
Alternative workbook trim: 7 x 10 in
Body font: Source Serif 4
Headings, boxes, captions, tables: Source Sans 3
Layout: single-column explanation with recurring operational boxes
Line length target: 60-75 average characters
```

## Rationale
- The book contains explanations, maps, tables, worksheets, checklists and exercises.
- A novel-style format would compress tables and workbook tools.
- A4/letter is too large for a commercial book unless it becomes a pure workbook.
- 17 x 24 cm gives a professional manual feel and enough room for Bando Decoder, case grids, profile maps and appendices.
